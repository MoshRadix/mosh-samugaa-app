About Addu Tides

High Tide — peak water level

Shaded region — night hours (18:00–06:00)

Addu City (Gan) has a mixed, mainly semidiurnal tide pattern — typically 2 highs and 2 lows per day, with a tidal range of ~0.8–1.2 m. Data uses harmonic constituents tuned to the Gan tidal station (IHO/UHSLC).
